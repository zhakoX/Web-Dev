Spaghetti Carbonara

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 400g
Pancetta 150g
Eggs 4 large
Parmesan Cheese 100g
Black Pepper To taste

Step 1: Do something.

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente.
  2. Fry the pancetta in a pan until crispy.
  3. Whisk the eggs with grated Parmesan cheese.
  4. Drain the spaghetti, saving a cup of cooking water.
  5. Combine the pasta with pancetta and the egg mixture. Add cooking water as needed.
  6. Season with black pepper and serve immediately.

Classic Pancakes

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 200g
Milk 300ml
Eggs 2 large
Sugar 2 tbsp
Butter For frying

Steps:

  1. Mix flour, sugar, and a pinch of salt in a bowl.
  2. Whisk eggs and milk together, then combine with dry ingredients.
  3. Heat a non-stick pan and melt a little butter.
  4. Pour in the batter and cook until bubbles appear, then flip.
  5. Serve with your favorite toppings.

Greek Salad

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Tomatoes 4 medium
Cucumber 1 large
Red Onion 1 small
Feta Cheese 200g
Olives 100g

Steps:

  1. Chop the tomatoes, cucumber, and red onion.
  2. Combine in a bowl with feta cheese and olives.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and season with oregano and salt.
  4. Serve chilled.
